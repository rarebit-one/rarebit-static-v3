## Visual Enhancements in rarebit-static-v3

This week, noticeable updates were made to the [rarebit-static-v3](https://github.com/rarebit-one/rarebit-static-v3) project, concentrating on its visual elements. Notable pull requests include the [introduction of a neon agent sentinel as the home hero image](https://github.com/rarebit-one/rarebit-static-v3/pull/97) and [adjustments to the hero crop for desktop readability](https://github.com/rarebit-one/rarebit-static-v3/pull/98). These changes aim to enhance the visual appeal and user interaction with the site.

## OAuth Management in standard_id

The team also released updates focusing on improving OAuth and session management within the [standard_id](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_id) repository. The [v0.25.0 release](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_id/releases/tag/v0.25.0) specifically enhances the persistence of browser session cookies across restarts, coupled with adjustments in passwordless authentication and bypass code controls as seen in [PR 245](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_id/pull/245) and [PR 244](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_id/pull/244).
