## OAuth Updates in standard_id

This week marked significant progress in OAuth enhancements in the [standard_id](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_id) repository. Two notable additions include the [introduction of per-audience token rate limits](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_id/pull/238) at the `/oauth/token` endpoint and the [release of version 0.23.0](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_id/releases/tag/v0.23.0), further advancing its OAuth and session management functionalities.

## Design Enhancements in rarebit-static-v3

The [rarebit-static-v3](https://github.com/rarebit-one/rarebit-static-v3) repository saw updates in design and layout. A fresh look was achieved through [differentiated page layouts and brand imagery](https://github.com/rarebit-one/rarebit-static-v3/pull/58) across the site, ensuring a more cohesive and visually appealing experience for users.

## Migration of Documentation

In a move aligned with organisational changes, luminality-web references were migrated to luminalityai in our documentation, as documented in the [.github](https://github.com/rarebit-one/.github/pull/39) repository.

## Private System Operations

Over the past week, across private systems, 260 runs were executed, maintaining an 80% green run rate.

Learn more about how this site is maintained through automation in our [detailed explanation](https://rarebit.one/field-notes/this-site-was-built-by-the-farm/).
