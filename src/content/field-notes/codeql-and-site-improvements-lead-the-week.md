## CodeQL Integration Across Repositories

This week featured an extensive rollout of CodeQL Actions-workflow scans across multiple repositories, enhancing security measures. The [standard_ledger](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_ledger/pull/36), [standard_singpass](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_singpass/pull/20), and [standard_id-google](https://github.com/rarebit-one/standard_id-google/pull/57) are just a few where these improvements were implemented. Adding CodeQL integration helps in identifying potential vulnerabilities and ensuring code robustness.
